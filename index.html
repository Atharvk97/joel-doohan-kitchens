Meet Joel Doohan

A rare find: A professional installer who realizes your dreams.

Based in Timperley, Altrincham, Joel Doohan has built a reputation over the past decade as a master kitchen craftsman. Pointing out high-street corporate suppliers who drop prefabricated units at your door, Joel treats every kitchen renovation as a unique piece of layout architecture.

"He listens to exactly what you want, brings invaluable spatial design expertise to the table, and executes with precision detail."

From rerouting plumbing and intricate electrical configuration to implementing custom cabinet spray-finishes, nothing is too much trouble. It is exactly why local homeowners choose to wait months just to secure Joel's dedicated personal service.
